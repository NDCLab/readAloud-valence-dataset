The great horned owl is a nocturnal bird native to the Americas. It is easily recognized by its tufted feather “horns,” called plumicorns, and its large, yellow eyes. A symbol of strength, courage, and beauty, the great horned owl has an unmistakable hoot, four to five syllables in length.
