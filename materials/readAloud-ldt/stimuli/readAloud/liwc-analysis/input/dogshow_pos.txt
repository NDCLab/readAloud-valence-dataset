In theory, this indicates that the dog has an ideal appearance and is in excellent health. Breed standards often use terms like “alert,” “intelligent,” and “energetic” because the intent is to maintain the integrity of the breed. Pups are carefully groomed for shows and prance around the ring to show off their stellar physique. Show training is intense and requires lots of love and determination to teach a dog how to bring home the gold.
