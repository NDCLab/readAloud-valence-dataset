It is caused by sulfur-containing compounds that actually interfere with iodine absorption in the body. So, although these vegetables may curb your risk of cancer and heart disease, you should avoid consuming too much, especially if you suffer from an iodine deficiency or other thyroid problems. The thyroid is a gland; it uses iodine to make hormones that control your metabolism. Today, the widespread availability of iodized table counteracts iodine deficiencies.
